GoWest:

  • Provides unobtrusive directions to any loaction of a city.
  • Supports orientation without disengagement from the environment.
  • Shows hotspots for bars, shops, and restaurants.
  • Allows to create a tour with up to 8 locations.
  • Shows apps related to your current location or a location on the map.
